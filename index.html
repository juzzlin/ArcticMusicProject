ARCTIC MUSIC PROJECT

Biography

Arctic Music Project is a Finnish musician who produces melodic synth pop. The frontman is the self-proclaimed celebrity Antero Teknomättö - the long-lost cousin of David Guetta, missing since the 1800s.

Antero Teknomättö was born to be a software engineer, and he uses a lot of his self-made MIDI sequencer, Noteahead. He also mainly uses Open Source software like Audacity and LSP plugins. He mixes with an X32 Producer and uses several hardware synthesizers such as the Korg Minilogue XD, the Behringer DeepMind 6, and the Waldorf Blofeld.

When it comes to drums, classic and analog 909 sounds never fail to impress.
