ARCTIC MUSIC PROJECT

Biography

Arctic Music Project is a Finnish musician who produces melodic synth pop. The frontman is the self-proclaimed celebrity Antero Teknomättö - the long-lost cousin of David Guetta, missing since the 1800s.

#house #trance #techno #synthpop

It all started small - and still is!
